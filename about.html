Our Heritage

Moulding Lives for Greatness Since Inception

His Grace Nursery and Primary School was founded on a vision to bridge the educational gap in Odeda local setting by providing standard, affordable, and faith-centered early childhood schooling. Located in the serene, welcoming community of Agbugburu Village, Abeokuta, Ogun State, we have grown to become a benchmark for discipline, academic achievement, and Christian guidance.

We prioritize individual student uniqueness. From our Montessori creche levels up to Primary Grade 6 graduating lines, we ensure our instructors build children who excel academically and carry deep moral values that reflect positive transformations within their local and broader communities.

"The fear of God and rigorous scientific exploration is the dual tool that guides all operations at His Grace Academy. We never compromise on standard child-welfare safety."

Core Anchors

Mission, Vision & Core Objectives

Our operational architecture is guided by specific goals, aligning parents, teachers, and school board directors under one unified direction.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures academic excellence, moral values, and lifelong learning for every child in Abeokuta and Ogun State.

Our Vision

To raise responsible, confident, and academically sound students who will positively impact society and excel as community leaders of tomorrow.

Our Values

Strict Discipline, Godly Conduct, Integrity in All Walks, Innovative Critical Thinking, Compassion, and Outstanding Mutual Kindness.
