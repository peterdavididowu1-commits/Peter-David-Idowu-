Welcome to DIMABIN

Preparing Leaders with Spiritual Depth and Academic Integrity

Welcome to the Divine Mandate Bible Institute (DIMABIN), an interdenominational citadel of learning dedicated to cultivating a deep theological understanding, uncompromised spiritual devotion, and practical ministry capacity.

At DIMABIN, we hold the absolute authority of Scripture as our highest standard. Our dynamic learning atmosphere bridges profound biblical study with realistic training, raising graduates who are capable of responding effectively to modern global ministry challenges while maintaining an unwavering moral stance.

Our Foundations

Vision & Mission

The spiritual coordinates guiding our educational mandate

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized interdenominational Bible institute, producing spiritually robust, intellectually equipped, and morally sound Christian leaders who will propagate the Gospel of Christ with absolute integrity and power across all nations.

  • Fostering global spiritual revivals
  • Restoring ethical standards in church leadership
  • Cultivating academic theological excellence

Our Mission

To deliver top-tier biblical and theological education through comprehensive training, mentoring, and practical service, instilling the fear of God as the core foundation for a successful, scandal-free Christian ministry and professional life.

  • Sound teaching based on biblical authority
  • Hands-on ministerial apprenticeship programs
  • Building standard, professional-level leadership skillsets
