Smart Admissions Portal

Simple, organized, and transparent enrollment steps for prospective parents wishing to join HGS.

Process Steps

Admissions Guidelines

1

Submit Online Registration

Fill the interactive Admission Form on this screen with accurate child and guardian details. Double-check email addresses and telephone numbers.

2

Administrative Follow-Up

Within 24 business hours, our Registrar office calls the parent to agree on a physical tour schedule, verify grade placement, and hand over the prospectus doc sheet.

3

Diagnostic Assessment

The child takes a friendly, conversational academic assessment focusing on writing, language reasoning, and basic mathematics structures to secure proper level placements.

4

Enrollment Confirmation

Upon review, the admission letter is generated. Secure bank payment of tuition and uniforms activates school supplies, textbooks, and the formal classroom roster listing.

Age Range Placements

- Creche / Toddler Care: 3 Months – 1.5 Years
- Playgroup / Pre-Nursery: 1.5 Years – 3 Years
- Nursery 1-2: 3 Years – 5 Years
- Primary 1-6: 5 Years and Above
