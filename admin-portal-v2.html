Administrator Portal V2

The centralized governance center of His Grace School. This system manages academic processing pipelines, monitors registration, records operator trails, and handles parent communications securely.

  • Active Admissions Workflow: Review student enrollment documents and issue Admission Numbers seamlessly.
  • Student Profiles & Password Directory: View registration files and portal security passwords instantly.
  • Parent Communications Console: Write and send SMTP email responses directly from the dashboard inbox.
  • Security Trail Registry: Real-time operations logging preserves tamper-resistant logs of administrative events.

Access to this portal is restricted solely to authorized His Grace School operators. All operational transactions, password retrievals, and admissions changes are tracked in real-time. Unauthorized attempts to gain access are violation offenses and will be instantly locked and logged.
